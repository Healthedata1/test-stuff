Extension: US Core Ethnicity Extension

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition/us-core-ethnicity Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2023-10-17 Computable Name: USCoreEthnicityExtension

Concepts classifying the person into a named category of humans sharing common history, traits, geographical origin or nationality. The ethnicity codes used to represent these concepts are based upon the Race & Ethnicity - CDC (CDCREC) which includes over 900 concepts for representing race and ethnicity of which 43 reference ethnicity. The ethnicity concepts are grouped by and pre-mapped to the 2 OMB ethnicity categories:

  • Hispanic or Latino
  • Not Hispanic or Latino.

Complies with 2015 Edition Common Clinical Data Set for patient race.

Context of Use

    This extension may be used on the following element(s)

    • Element ID: Patient
    • Element ID: RelatedPerson
    • Element ID: Person
    • Element ID: Practitioner
    • Element ID: FamilyMemberHistory

Summary

Complex Extension: Concepts classifying the person into a named category of humans sharing common history, traits, geographical origin or nationality. The ethnicity codes used to represent these concepts are based upon the Race & Ethnicity - CDC (CDCREC) which includes over 900 concepts for representing race and ethnicity of which 43 reference ethnicity. The ethnicity concepts are grouped by and pre-mapped to the 2 OMB ethnicity categories:

  • Hispanic or Latino
  • Not Hispanic or Latino.

  • ombCategory: Coding: The 2 ethnicity category codes according to the OMB Standards for Maintaining, Collecting, and Presenting Federal Data on Race and Ethnicity, Statistical Policy Directive No. 15, as revised, October 30, 1997.
  • detailed: Coding: The 41 CDC ethnicity codes that are grouped under one of the 2 OMB ethnicity category codes.
  • text: string: Plain text representation of the ethnicity concept(s).
